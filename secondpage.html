Drama Viewers Rating Report: 2nd Week of October

24th October, 2016

This week saw the end of buzz drama Moonlight Drawn By Clouds, which pulled in an impressive 23.3% and 22.9% for its final two episodes, respectively. The only Big 3 broadcast channel competition it faced on Monday was Woman With a Suitcase, since Moon Lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo was pre-empted at the last minute for a baseball game.

That pre-emption seemed to cause some confusion in the international sphere, but everything was right back on track for the show on Tuesday. Unfortunately, the day off didn’t do the show any favors in terms of ratings, since it pulled in a very moderate 5.9%. It’s Drinking Solo over on cable that’s been getting better and better, managing to pull in in 4.4% with its even episode this week.

But the real surprise seems to be coming from the middle of the week, with Shopping King Louis looking poised to possibly overtake Jealousy Incarnate. That really wasn’t something I was expecting, especially considering the head start Jealousy got, but it’s fun to have some shakeups now and then.

Over on the weekend, Fantastic finished up its poignant run at 2.4%, while tvN’s The K2 keeps flirting with that 6% mark. Maybe one day, maybe never.
